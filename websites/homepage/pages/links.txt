<h3>General Starting points</h3>
<a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/">National Center for Biotechnology Information</a> - Literature, Sequences, Tools<br>
<a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Entrez/">Entrez cross-database search</a> - Cross database search (Google for life-sciences)<br><br>

<h3>DNA/RNA analysis</h3>
<a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Tools/">Tools available at NCBI</a> - Various tools like: Genome (Sequence analysis), Protein (Sequence & Structure analysis).<br>
<a href="http://www.ensembl.org/index.html">Ensemble database</a> - The Ensembl project produces genome databases for vertebrates and other eukaryotic species.<br><br>

<h3>PROTEIN analysis</h3>
<a href="www.pdb.org/">PDB</a> - The PDB archive: information about proteins, nucleic acids<br>
<a href="http://www.expasy.org/">ExPASy molecular biology server</a> - The ExPASy server (Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics)<br><br>

<h3>METABOLITE analysis</h3>
<a href="http://www.chemspider.com/">ChemSpider</a> - Providing access to millions of chemical structures<br>
<a href="http://www.hmdb.ca/">HMDB</a> - The Human Metabolome Database (HMDB)<br><br>

<h3>PATHWAY</h3>
<a href="http://www.genome.jp/kegg/">KEGG: Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes</a> - Pathway & other information <br><br>
