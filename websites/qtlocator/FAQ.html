Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel the script, will it save the results I have so far?
Yes you can cancel at any time during the QTL mapping phase. So at 5 pm turn off the computer and go home. When returning the next day just restart the analysis and the phenotypes already analysed will be skipped

Where do I get more help?
Contact one of the authors by email, the email adresses are on the Home page
